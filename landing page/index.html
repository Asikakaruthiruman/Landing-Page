Music is an art form that uses sound organized in time.
It's found in every culture and society, serving various purposes like entertainment, artistic expression, cultural preservation, and communication.
Music can be created using vocal sounds, instruments, or electronic devices.
It encompasses a wide range of genres, styles, and forms, from classical symphonies to pop songs, jazz improvisations to electronic beats.
Throughout history, music has evolved, influenced by cultural, technological, and social changes, reflecting the diversity and creativity of human expression.
